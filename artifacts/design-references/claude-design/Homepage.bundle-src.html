02 — About

Trained in three cities. Practicing in one.

Dr. Tsolaridis completed his ENT residency at the University of Athens, followed by fellowships in rhinology in Paris and head & neck surgery in Vienna.

Since 2003 he has led a private practice on Alexandras Avenue, alongside operating privileges at two Athens hospitals. He is a member of the Hellenic and European ENT societies.

From the article FAQs

Most-asked, straight-answered.

Pulled from the accordion section of our most-read articles.

Is septoplasty painful? How much time off work? +

Most patients describe it as strong pressure rather than pain. Expect 3–5 days at home, and full exercise at two weeks.

My child snores loudly — when should we see an ENT? +

If snoring is nightly, loud, or interrupted by pauses — or your child is tired in the day — it's worth a consultation.

Do you offer a second-opinion service on scans? +

Yes — upload your CT/MRI and clinical notes via the Online Visit form and expect a written opinion within 48h.
